Project aim

It aims towards providing user a means to undertake an informed decision of which stocks should they invest in. Although stock market is fickle, investor can overcome the skepticism about the right stock by gaining its past information and roughly analyzing its future.Our project aims to reduce this labour by analyzing and predicting the future os a stock. In other words the knowledge of whether a stock goes up or down is just one click away.

Project Functionalities

  • DASHBOARD:
    It allows user to search for a NASDAQ/NSE stock and view the recent trends of the stock along with the OCHLV indicators.

  • PREDICT THE FUTURE:
    User can view the closing value of a desired/searched NASDAQ/NSE stock for the next 7 days and visualize the model accuracy.

  • NEWS AND TWEETS SENTIMENT:
    User gets the current news and tweets about a desired stock in just one click. Also, the model provides user with the overall sentiment of a given stock based on tweets, to allow him/her to make an informed decision.

  • KNOW MORE ABOUT STOCKS:
    A novice user with a little or no knowledge about stock market and stocks in general can refer info provided here to get a clear idea.

  • VIEW LIVE STOCKS:
    It allows user to visualize the NASDAQ/NSE stocks updated to the recent 5 or more seconds.
